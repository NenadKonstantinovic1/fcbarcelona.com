Essential information

FC Barcelona, located in Barcelona, Spain, was founded on November 29, 1899. They play in Blaugrana (blue and garnet) kits, a long tradition that spans to the early 20th century. Their stadium is the Camp Nou, which ranks first in stadium capacity in Europe. The supporters, locally known as Culés, attend as much games as possible and cheer like there's no tomorrow. FC Barcelona's unique and infamous tiki-taka playstyle earned them a place among the giants of European football. La Masia, Barcelona's youth academy, has produced many talented young players.

Més que un club - More than a club

FC Barcelona is not just a football team. It is a symbol of Catalonia's passion, tradition and pride. It has been recognized for its unique playing style, legendary history and the unshakable loyalty of its supporters.

24/25 Season Summary

FC Barcelona had a very successful season. At the end, the club won three trophies - Spanish Super Cup, Copa del Rey and the Spanish La Liga, simultaneously achieving the domestic treble. The players didn't disappoint either, dropping amazing performances day in, day out. The club is entering the upcoming 25/26 season with a lot of expectation, mainly targeting a return to glory in the UEFA Champions League.
