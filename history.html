The Beginning of FC Barcelona

Joan Gamper, a swiss football executive, alongside a dozen other football enthusiasts, came together and formed the club on November 29, 1899. The year after, the infamous Blaugrana kit was first assembled and used in an official match. The club then started competing in Spain's first division of football, marking the start of a journey that continues to this very day.
